Format

Zilliqa office, thebridge, #01-03 Ascent Building
  1. 1

    30-min Presentation or Video

    A presenter (usually the author of the paper) will summarize key ideas and concepts in the paper. Conference video may be played if author cannot attend in person.

  2. 2

    45-min Breakout Discussion Groups

    Session will break out into smaller discussion groups to go over papers critically with an eye to research extensions or critiques.

  3. 3

    15min: Presentations and Sharing

    Quick, rapid-fire sharing of discussion findings with the community.
